Bridging the gap…

How many times have you had an Idea for a business, what did you do next? There are countless stories of ideas that never took off, or those that fall off the cliff immediately after the startup. We often hear that if you follow your heart and passion, you could grow a business idea. Any entrepreneur will however point out the huge chasm between the dream and the walk! If the idea was all you needed, we would have had much more success than we currently have.

Your complete resource hub

As special as your business Idea is, there are various common resources that every business that stands the test of time need; the guidance of others who have walked your path, directions and recommendations on where to get web site designers, or the accountant that makes the numbers tell a story. Instead of running around and experimenting with resources, StartUp brings all these resources together in one single space. You will be surprised how much resources are available to make it rather difficult not to succeed
